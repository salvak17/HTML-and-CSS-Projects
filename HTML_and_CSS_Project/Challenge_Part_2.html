Fort Hood

Fort Hood is a US Army post located in Killeen, Texas. It is the site of Vanessa Guillen's death, where she was bludgeoned to death with a hammer in the armory room where she worked. Her body was then transported from the military installation and taken to be burned and butchered to get rid of her body. Her murder was the day before she was to report sexual harassment, when the killer found out she was going to come forward, he murdered her with a hammer, and had other accomplices. An investigation was not opened soon enough, and only started going after Vanessa's mother and family went to social media to demand they find her daughter as she was missing since April. A petition to hold the US Army accountable to fail to keep their own safe and failed to find justice is under way. Vanessa is not the first victim at Fort Hood and her body was found days after Gregory Wedel-Morales, who was found missing LAST YEAR while driving in Killeen. There have been more disappearances from Fort Hood within the past year. These men and women vowed to keep our country safe, it is time the US Army help their soldiers stay safe.
